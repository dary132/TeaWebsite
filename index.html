The Prana Ritual

Tea as Practice

Every blend is designed to accompany a specific moment on your mat — or off it. We believe the act of brewing tea is the practice: presence, patience, and breath in a cup.

Source herbs ethically. Steep with intention. Sip with awareness.

Learn Our Philosophy
01

Set Your Intention

Choose a blend that matches your energy or where you want to go.

02

Heat Mindfully

Bring water to the right temperature. Presence begins before the kettle whistles.

03

Steep & Breathe

3–5 minutes. Use this time to breathe, stretch, or simply be.

04

Receive

Drink slowly. Let the herbs meet you where you are.
