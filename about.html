Our Story

Rooted in Practice

Born on a yoga mat in Portland, Oregon. Brewed with devotion ever since.

How Prana Tea Began

It started with a problem: after a long Ashtanga practice, our founder Nadia couldn't find a tea that felt like a natural extension of the practice. Everything was either too stimulating, too medicinal, or simply... ordinary.

So she went back to her roots — literally. Drawing on her background in Ayurveda and a decade of studying herbalism, Nadia began blending for herself, then for her students, then for anyone who wanted a cup of something more intentional.

Today, Prana Tea blends are crafted in small batches in our Portland studio, using certified organic herbs sourced directly from small farms we trust. Every blend is designed to accompany a state of practice — not just a time of day.

"Tea isn't what you drink before yoga. Tea is yoga — when done with presence."

— Nadia Okonkwo, Founder
