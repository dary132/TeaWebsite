物語 · Our Story

Rooted in Practice

Born on a yoga mat in Portland, Oregon. Inspired by the Japanese teahouse tradition. Brewed with devotion ever since.

How Prana Tea Began

It started with a problem: after a long Ashtanga practice, our founder Nadia couldn't find a tea that felt like a natural extension of the work. Everything was either too stimulating, too medicinal, or simply ordinary.

After studying Ayurveda in India and spending time at traditional Japanese teahouses in Kyoto, Nadia returned home with a new vision — tea not as a beverage, but as a practice. A moment of stillness, intention, and embodied presence.

Today, Prana Tea blends are crafted in small batches in our Portland studio. Every blend is designed to accompany a state of practice — drawing equally from yogic tradition and the Japanese concept of ichigo ichie — one time, one meeting.

"Each cup is a ceremony. Each sip, unrepeatable."

— Nadia Okonkwo, Founder
